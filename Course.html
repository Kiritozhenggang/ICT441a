ICT60315 - Advanced Diploma of Information Technology Business Analysis

This course provides individuals with the skills and knowledge to conduct business analysis, identify and coordinate relevant ICT business solutions in the Information and Communication Technology (ICT) environment to achieve specific organizational goals.

ICT60415 - Advanced Diploma of Information Technology Project Management

This course provides individuals with the skills and knowledge to manage a range of information and communication technology projects to meet customer requirements. The project may cover a wide range of specialties, including software development, hardware procurement and implementation, network analysis and deployment, and network and/or system development

ICT60220 - Advanced Diploma of Information Technology

This course reflects the role of individuals with professional technical skills or rich experience in managing business and personnel management skills in various information and communication technology (ICT) positions.

ICA60411 - Advanced Diploma of Information Technology Project Management

This course provides individuals with the skills and knowledge to manage a range of information and communication technology projects to meet customer requirements. The project may cover a wide range of specialties, including software development, hardware procurement and implementation, network analysis and deployment, and network and/or system development.

ICT60215 - Advanced Diploma of Network Security

This course provides individuals with the skills and knowledge to plan, design, manage, and monitor enterprise ICT networks as independent ICT experts or as part of a team responsible for advanced ICT Internet Security Systems. This qualification has a high-level foundation in information and communication technology, an appropriate security department, and the ability to specialize in multiple fields such as voice, wireless, network infrastructure, and sustainability.
